Colour

Colour, in a simple definition, is light reflected by an object.
Color can affect how people feel and is symbolic. In art, a color theory exists.
Color theory includes the colour wheel, colour value, and colour schemes.
Color is a part of all art and is all around us

Typography

Typography is the art of arranging letters and text in a way that makes the copy legible, clear,
and visually appealing to the reader. Typography involves font style, appearance, and structure,
which aims to elicit certain emotions and convey specific messages.
